Work in Insurance? 3 Ways Using Symphony Can Help You

Harry Patz

Those who work in the Insurance industry are no strangers to legacy processes & workflows which often lead to communication breakdown, slow response time, and a lot of frustration. The good news is that there is now a modern solution with enterprise-ready security & compliance required by the industry.

Insurance professionals - whether a portfolio manager, field agent or claims officer - can leverage modern technology like Symphony to improve their day-to-day operations. See how three types of different Insurance employees can all benefit from the power of Symphony:

  1. Portfolio managers can follow common #hashtags to organize & track specific events. During extreme weather events, evidenced by the 2017 hurricane season, including  Hurricane Harvey, Maria, and Irma, it is  critical to absorb all of the latest critical information. On Symphony, PMs can track weather reports, updates, and news stories in real time with tags and in-platform content applications. As an alert is received, a persistent chat room can be created with the necessary analysts and colleagues, so important information does not get lost in the shuffle.
  2. Field agents can use Symphony mobile to stay connected & efficient, wherever they are. Field agents are quickly deployed to assess the damage from severe events  like the recent California wildfires. Even seasoned field agents can be overwhelmed by the people, sites, and information flowing in around them. With Symphony mobile, agents can communicate instantly with colleagues at HQ to make sure they ask the right questions, and gather all of the necessary photos and information for their clients’ claims.
  3. Claims officers can use real-time chat to process claims quickly & accurately. Fragmented communication is one of the top reasons why claims aren’t processed as soon as desired. Claims officers can use Symphony to set up chat rooms for specific clients or cases to process claims quickly and accurately. Chat rooms which include customers can alleviate clients’ frustration by immediately relaying questions or concerns to the right customer service representatives at HQ via a real-time chat room. See who has read the message with timestamps, receive faster responses, and create actionable insights. Faster claim resolution delivers a better customer experience, driving a higher Net Promoter Score, one of the best predictors of future company success.

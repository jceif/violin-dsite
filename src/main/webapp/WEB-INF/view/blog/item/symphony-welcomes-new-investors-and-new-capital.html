Symphony Welcomes New Investors and New Capital

David Gurle

Last week marked the one-year anniversary of the formation of Symphony.

Not satisfied with the state of communication products that is making our life more complicated, we set out to design what became the Symphony platform. The company was founded with the desire to transform the way users communicate effectively and securely within a single workflow application. Today, this is a reality. We are here to challenge the status quo and deliver a unique solution that brings communication, community and content into one powerful and rich workflow platform. Today, I am delighted to announce that our vision, technology and customer momentum helped us cement more than $100 million in new capital from Google Inc., Lakestar, Natixis, Societe Generale and UBS with a group of existing investors, including Silicon Valley venture firm Merus Capital. This investment will help to further accelerate our worldwide expansion and user adoption.

The question you may be asking is why are these investors making such a big commitment in Symphony. While I can’t speak for them, I would like to share some insights from our conversations. First and foremost, we have taken a unique architectural approach. Our cloud-based platform is designed from the ground up to protect customers’ information from cyber-threats with our end-to-end security architecture, where encryption infrastructure is owned and operated by customers. Our business model is simply about connectivity in one-to-one and to one-to-many communications from a single workflow platform. Symphony is built for highly regulated businesses and enterprises that need to meet regulatory compliance requirements, with a passion for intuitive user experience and engaging design. These are just a few takeaways.

Furthermore, with our recent public launch of the platform – we now have more than 40,000 users in 100 countries (the 100th country from which we have users is Cote D’Ivoire!) and have booked more than $10M in service orders in just a few months.

$100+ million is more than what we anticipated to raise! This amount gives us the runway to stay focused on our vision of becoming the standard for business communications and allow us to accelerate our growth globally. We will increase our product development team, as well as fill our sales and account management positions globally to meet the very high customer demand at our doors.

We are privileged to have the support of our new and existing investors to make Symphony a reality and empower users to communicate effectively and securely. I am also very proud of the Symphony team. Without their tireless effort to build trust with our customers and deliver the best product and service, this investment would not have been possible. Lastly, I want to thank our loyal customers and partners for your continued support as we work to deliver what you need in terms of productivity improvements.

Feel free to connect with me on Symphony, I look forward to our continued dialogue.

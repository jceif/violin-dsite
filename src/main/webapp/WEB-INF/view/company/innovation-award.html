Announcing the Symphony Innovation Award

Updated August 30th

The Symphony Innovation Award recognizes cutting-edge innovations that positively impact the community of users on Symphony. Our partners and customers are continuously developing new applications and bots to improve productivity and collaboration.

How to Enter

Individuals or teams must provide a submission to qualify for the Symphony Innovation Award. Symphony will select and announce winners biannually. Please contact innovationaward@symphony.com for questions.

Prizes & Recognition

  • Announcement on Symphony website, social media platforms, newsletters, and/or press
  • Award presentation on stage at Symphony Innovate
  • Free entry to the annual Symphony Innovate conference (Hong Kong or NY) to all winning developers & team members

Judging Panel

The judging panel will include representatives from Symphony.

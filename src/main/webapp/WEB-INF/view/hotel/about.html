About Vipp hotel

An untraditional hotel

A self-service concept

The Vipp hotel is self-service concept. Our hotel is not ordinary, and instead of one location with identical rooms and a continental breakfast, we offer experiences and essentials. Our rooms are spread out at various locations, so we do not have a reception or a concierge. You won't find a 24-hour lobby, a gym, daily housekeeping or fitness facilities.

Instead, we offer tailored and curated design destinations out of the ordinary, filled with Vipp's take on design. A part of the experience of our destinations is to engage with the interior of our hotel rooms. Every room is equipped with a kitchen just waiting to be used, so when you have done your grocery shopping, start cooking and enjoy.

 

Carefully considered amenities

The rooms  in the hotel are stocked with carefully considered amenities. In the mini bar is water and a select bottle of champagne, and in the bathroom you will find care products by Aèsop. Bedding in organic cotton from Danish Aiayu and organic cotton towels from Vipp are ready to embrace you.

Should you have questions or need assistance, a member of the Vipp team will be standby to assist you.

FAQ about the Vipp hotel

Where is the Vipp hotel located?

New rooms are always in the making at the Vipp hotel. So far, we have two rooms available. Get out of the city and into nature at the 55m2 Vipp shelter at lake Immeln in Sweden, or go for maximized urbanity in the 400m2 Vipp loft in Copenhagen. Or stay tuned for our next room opening early 2018; a restored landmark in Northern Copenhagen, the Chimney house.  Explore all the rooms here.

How do I get to the Vipp shelter or the Vipp loft from the airport?

Vipp shelter: If you are going to the shelter, we recommend you to fly to Malmö and rent a car at the airport and drive to the shelter. It is a drive of approx. 127 km. If you fly in to Copenhagen the drive will be approx. 150 km. 

Vipp loft: From Copenhagen Airport to the Vipp loft is 15 minutes by car or 15 minutes by metro followed by a 10-minute walk.

When you have booked one of our rooms, we will be in touch to provide you with directions and other practical information for your stay at the Vipp hotel.

What is the price for a night at the Vipp hotel?

Every room at the Vipp hotel is a unique experience, so the rooms have different rates.

Vipp shelter: 600.00 EUR per night for max 2 adults.
Vipp loft: 1,000.00 EUR per night for max 4 adults. 

How many people can stay in your rooms?

All rooms in the Vipp hotel are for adults over the age of 16 only, no children and no pets.

Vipp shelter: max 2 adults, one queen size bed.
Vipp loft: max 4 adults, two queen size beds.

How big are the rooms?

Vipp shelter: The shelter is a 55m2 pod wrapped in the Vipp DNA from head to toe; every detail envisioned by Vipp Chief Designer, Morten Bo Jensen.

Vipp loft: The loft is 400m2 of design and art intertwined, remodeled by Danish David Thulstrup Studio.

What is included in a stay?

When you stay at the Vipp hotel, you are welcomed by a location with all the necessities. The refrigerator is stocked with bottled water and a bottle of champagne, and tea and coffee is ready to be brewed.

Do you have room service?

We do not offer room service or a restaurant, but there is a fully equipped kitchen just waiting to be used. Information on nearby grocery stores, cafés and restaurants will be sent to you prior to your stay.

What are the check in and check out times (UTC+01)? 

Vipp loft: Check in 15:00, check out 15:00.
Vipp shelter: Check in 12:00 noon, check out 15:00.

Late check out can be requested upon reservation and is subject to a late check out fee of EUR 75. Vipp reserves the right to decline requests for late check out.

How do I pick up the keys?

We don't have a reception, but we have taken care of everything for your arrival at the Vipp hotel. 

Vipp shelter: The shelter awaits your arrival. Park your car just out front and make your way to the shelter. Further instructions will be sent to you before your stay.

Vipp loft: A representative from Vipp will be ready to welcome you at the Vipp loft and give you a brief tour. 

What is your cancellation policy?

All bookings at the Vipp hotel are non-refundable. If you cannot make it for your booked reservation, we do offer the option of changing the date of your stay according to availability. A second cancellation cannot be refunded or rescheduled.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is street parking just outside the Vipp loft, and free parking by the Vipp shelter. 

Do you have WiFi?

Free WiFi is available.

Is smoking allowed?

The Vipp hotel is a smoke-free property, which also counts e-cigarettes. All rooms and public areas are designated as such. A smoking fee of 1000 EUR per day will be assessed if evidence of smoking is found in your room or on the premises.

How do I pay?

Full pre-payment by credit card or bank transfer at the time of reservation. A valid credit card is required to confirm your booking.

 

We are here to help

If you have questions about your booking at the Vipp hotel, please contact us at hotel@vipp.com
